Our Services

Our goal is to help individuals with the referencing and prescription management process. Receiving a prescription is a necessary part of visiting the hospital for anyone. Prescriptions aren't always the same and differ from hospital to hospital; some may be digital, while others may be handwritten. Because of this, managing these documents can be time-consuming and difficult because most individuals either forget about them or feel compelled to keep a file for them. Our goal is to address this issue by utilizing deep learning, OCR (Optical Character Recognition), and handwriting recognition to digitize and store prescriptions. This will enable us to provide a medical prescription manager that keeps track of all the prescriptions, relieving the user of their hardship. Every user only needs to utilize their device's camera to scan the paper once. How is this accomplished? Using deep learning techniques and algorithms like 'xyz,' we can properly predict terms like pharmaceutical name, dose, and other medical terms by applying a predefined vocabulary of medical terms. We can also effectively detect letters and whitespaces in handwritten or digital text.

What is an eScript

A digital adaptation of a paper script is called an eScript. Your doctor will provide it to you via text message or email. It looks like a QR code, commonly known as an electronic token.
